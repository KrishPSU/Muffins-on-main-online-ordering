Muffins on Main: Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 5, 2025

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information necessary to fulfill orders and provide our services, including your name, address, email, phone number, and payment information.

2. Use of Information

Your information is used solely for fulfilling your orders, processing payments, communicating order updates, promotional purposes (if opted in), and improving our services.

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary for payment processing, SMS delivery services, or when legally required by authorities.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Local Storage

Our online ordering system uses local storage to enhance user experience. You may disable local storage via your browser settings, although some site functionalities may be limited.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be clearly communicated through our website and, where applicable, via text message or email. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
